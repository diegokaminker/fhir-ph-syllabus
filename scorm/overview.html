Course Objectives

The goal of this Public Health FHIR Training is to equip participants with a comprehensive understanding of FHIR standards and their application in public health. By the end of the course, attendees will be able to:

  • Navigate FHIR specifications
  • Explain FHIR resources and profiles
  • Leverage interoperability to enhance public health data exchange
  • Implement FHIR in public health modernization efforts

Learning Outcomes

2 Days
Healthcare Professionals
Intermediate
  • Define interoperability in healthcare
  • Describe data modernization principles
  • Explain FHIR in-depth
  • Execute basic FHIR operations
  • Plan for FHIR implementation

Course Structure

2 Days
10 Sessions
7 Assignments

This course is structured over two intensive days with hands-on activities, presentations, and practical exercises. Each day focuses on specific aspects of FHIR implementation in public health contexts.

Prerequisites

Participants should have:

  • Basic understanding of healthcare IT systems
  • Familiarity with web technologies (HTML, JSON)
  • Experience with data exchange concepts
  • Interest in public health informatics
