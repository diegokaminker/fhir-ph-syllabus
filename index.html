HL7 FHIR Public Health Training 2025

This Syllabus is here: https://tinyurl.com/phfhir2025

Objectives

The goal of this Public Health FHIR Training is to equip participants with a comprehensive understanding of FHIR standards and their application in public health. By the end of the course, attendees will be able to navigate FHIR specifications, explain FHIR resources and profiles, and leverage interoperability to enhance public health data exchange and modernization efforts.

Themes and Topics

  • Interoperability in Healthcare
  • History of HL7 Standards
  • APIs and REST
  • Public Health Use Case
  • Navigating FHIR Core Spec
  • FHIR Resources & Profiles
  • Terminology Basics
  • Mapping & Operations
  • FHIR Search & Validation
  • FHIR Technologies & IGs
  • FHIR Project Team Roles
  • HL7 Development Process
  • HL7 Public Health WG

Learning Outcomes

  • Define interoperability
  • Describe data modernization
  • Explain FHIR in-depth
  • Execute basic FHIR operations
  • Plan for FHIR implementation

Methodology

A synchronous, virtual course with hands-on discovery work, combining presentations with group assignments and quizzes.
